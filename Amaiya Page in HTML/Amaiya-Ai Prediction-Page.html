How does AI Prediction work?

AI prediction uses historical data to estimate what's likely to happen next. By analyzing patterns in past sales, crop yields, weather, labor, and inventory, AI calculates probabilities and makes forecasts, such as future sales, crop yields, or inventory shortages. The more clean and organized the data, the more accurate the predictions. Over time, the system improves by comparing predictions to actual outcomes. For growers, AI prediction doesn't replace decision-making—it helps anticipate problems, save time, plan smarter, and reduce surprises.
